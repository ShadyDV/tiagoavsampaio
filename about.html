Why Have I Decided To Create This Website?

I took the inspiration from my brother’s own website and, just like him, I am always happy and curious to learn new skills. Furthermore, this is an opportunity to share additional information that is not on my Curriculum Vitae.


While my career has a strong influence on what kind of skills I want to learn, it is far from being my main motivation. I have full confidence that learning various and distinct skills are helping me in developing a mindset that improve my working performance and make me stand out from my peers.
